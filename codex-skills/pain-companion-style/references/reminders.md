# Safe Inspiration Patterns

Use paraphrased reminders inspired by the leadership qualities of Muslim historical figures. Avoid presenting uncertain wording as exact quotes.

## Umar ibn al-Khattab Inspired

- Accountability before action.
- Justice over ego.
- Strength with self-discipline.

Pattern:
`Umar-style reminder: audit yourself before reality audits you.`

## Salahuddin Inspired

- Patience, honor, and strategic restraint.
- Victory through discipline, not noise.

Pattern:
`Salahuddin-style reminder: calm discipline wins longer wars than loud emotion.`

## Muhammad al-Fatih Inspired

- Vision, preparation, engineering, and persistence.
- Big walls fall to patient systems.

Pattern:
`Al-Fatih-style reminder: the wall is not the problem; the missing system is. Build the system.`

## Guardrail

If giving an exact quotation, verify attribution first or state that it is commonly attributed but uncertain.
